Making the lasagne


Once you've made the white/cheese sauce and your pasta is rolled out you're ready to make the lasagne.


Ingredients
  • Ground beef/pork meat
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Celery
  • Optional: Red wine
  • Fresh pasta/dried lasagne sheets
  • White sauce/cheese sauce
  • Grated cheese
  • Salt/pepper

    • Method
    1. Dice the carrot, celery, onion and garlic then gently soften in a pan in that order so as to not overcook any of the vegetables
    2. Add gently brown the meat of your choice (some recipes use a mix of beef/pork/veal)
    3. Add a small amount of red wine and allow the alcohol to cook off as the meat and veg stew, now is a good time to season.
    4. If you're using fresh pasta cut it into sheets the same size as your baking dish
    5. Start with a layer of the meat sauce in the baking dish then a layer of pasta and then a layer of white/cheese sauce
    6. Make sure the final layer is the white/cheese sauce before covering with tinfoil and baking in the oven
    7. Once baked remove the foil and top with a layer of grated cheese before grilling/broiling until the cheese has melted and browned
    8. Make sure you leave the lasagne to stand for 10-15 minutes before serving, this will prevent the lasagne from falling apart as you serve

    That's it for the lasagne recipe. If you need the recipe for the sauce it can be found here.
