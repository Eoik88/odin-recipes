Making fresh pasta


Ingredients
  • flour
  • eggs

    • Steps
    1. Pour your flour into a bowl and make a well for the eggs.
    2. Crack your eggs into the flour
    3. Using a fork gradually whisk the eggs whilst slowly drawing flour into the eggs
    4. Once the pasta has come together enough switch to using your hands and continue kneeding the dough, your aiming for a consistancy similar to playdoh
    5. Wrap the pasta dough in clingfilm and let rest for half an hour before rolling out
    6. Using your pasta roller gradually work down through the settings until your pasta is the required thickness

    Use your fresh pasta in the next recipe a lasagne or you can cut the pasta into your desired shape for another recipe.
